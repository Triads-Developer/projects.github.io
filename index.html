Our Services

Our mission is to advance research that leverages the unprecedented resources of the computational age to address the most pressing social dilemmas of the 21st century.

Dashboards

The TRIADS team can build individualized dashboards tailored to the research data.

Data Collection and Scraping

TRIADS can develop a browser extension for data collection and web scraping, enabling users to gather research data from websites and automate data extraction.

Mobile App Development

The TRIADS team can provide mobile app development to create native iOS and Android apps that can supplement existing web-based systems or serve as standalone replacements.

Custom Machine Learning Models

TRIADS can help you organize research data and build machine-learning models to improve decision-making.

Database Development

TRIADS can help you develop tailored research database solutions.
