FAQ

  • TRIADS Frequently Asked Questions
  • What does TRIADS stand for?

    Transdisciplinary Institute in Applied Data Sciences

  • Can TRIADS help me with my project?

    We would love to help in any way we can with your work. We can build custom software, apps, software packages, and scripts. Our goal is to kickstart your project so you can then use it for your research. If it turns out that your needs are something we can't accomplish we will someone who can.

  • How does it work?

    Begin by filling out the Consultation Form. Upon submission, it will send an email to the TRIADS team. We will reach out to you to set up a time to discuss you and your work.

    Please come talk with us! We’d love to hear more about you and your work, and we can discuss the potential options.
